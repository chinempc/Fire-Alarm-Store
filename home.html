Who Are We?

AutoCaller specializes in providing customers with leading fire alarm & life safety technologies, application expertise, and services. By assembling a project team with vast industry experience and technical competence, AutoCaller works with their clients to deliver compliant, non-proprietary solutions which avoid sole-source dependency and assure long-term cost competitiveness. From system retrofit to new construction to turnkey system installation, AutoCaller provides the very best value for your project regardless of size and complexity. AutoCaller’s customer support programs ensure the performance and integrity of your system is maintained without compromise to occupant safety, business mission, and AHJ compliance.
